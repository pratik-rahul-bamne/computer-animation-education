Morphing & Warping Techniques

What is Morphing?

Morphing is a visual effect that smoothly transforms one image or shape into another through seamless transition of geometry and texture.

Key Techniques:

  • Cross-dissolving: Gradual opacity blending
  • Warping: Geometric transformation
  • Feature matching: Aligning key points
  • Interpolation: Smooth transition frames
